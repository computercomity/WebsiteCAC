Computer @nd Comity

We focus on Information Technology, share computer science knowledge, provide IT services.
We are in CUHK(SZ).

JOIN US !

We plan for the following types of activities

Discover Exciting Events

Seminar

In this regular activity, students are supposed to share their own study notes and skills of comnputer science. The seminar based on group cooperation can make study interesting and efficient.

Workshop

In this regular activity, a student will volunteer to be the lecturer. A topic about computer science will be covered each time. Then other participates are supposed to complete some problems to apply what they learned in class or C@C

Lecture

We will try to contact with professors to give speeches for students and solve their problems. We will also try to contact with those technology company executives to give speeches.

Event

We plan to hold big events on campus by cooperating
with related technology companies and associations.
This kind of events aims at the promotion of IT technology,

Competition

We will cooperate with the Academic Section of
Student Union to hold university computer competition
and select outstanding students to take part in
higher levels of competitions.
