We are in CUHK(SZ).

JOIN US !

We plan for the following types of activities

Discover Exciting Events

Seminar

We plan to develop a kind of regular activity in the form of seminars. In the seminars, students are supposed to share their special study skills and learning experience on computer science. The seminars will be based on group cooperation, which will surely make the study more interesting and efficient.

Workshop

We plan to hold some workshops as a kind of regular activity. Each time, a participant will volunteer to be the lecturer and share some knowledge about computer science. Then, all the participants are supposed to solve some related problems together and apply them into practical development.

Lecture

We will invite prestigious professors to deliver speeches on the latest topics and help students solve related problems. We will also try to invite executives from some famous science and technology cooperations to introduce their cutting-edge and fashionable technologies.

Event

We plan to hold various events with the support of some science and technology corporations. We aim at delivering the latest IT news, spreading computer science knowledge and cultivating IT environment among students. And we hope that the application of computer science can become a popular trend in our school.

Competition

We are going to reach a cooperative relationship with the Academic Department of Student Union and hold the University Computer Competitions with their help. Through selections, we will invite those outstanding students to take part in higher levels of competitions.
